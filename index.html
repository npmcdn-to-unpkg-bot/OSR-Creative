Our Process// Your Vision


  • 1. In Vino Veritas

    The Romans were on to something when they declared that "in wine, truth". The first step of this journey is to meet and talk about your baby, your company. Your website should be an expression of your core values and what you envision for the future of your company. At this meeting we'll talk about the type of clients you want to attract, what you imagine your website to be, any and all expectations, and most importantly we'll set up a communication plan.

  • 2. Feild trip

    This is the fun part for us! We want to experience the product or service that you offer. In order to accuratley represent you and your company online, we think it's essential to dive into the customer experience first hand. This stage will also include user and competitor research. You know what they say, keep your friends close and your enimes closer...

  • 3. Inspire Us

    We want to create a site that conveys the values and style that you had in mind when you created your company, so we gotta get inside your head. You'll create pintrest board of any and all colors, textures, sites, fonts, pictures, cute animals, or anything else that you want expressed on your site. Of course we can't incorporate it all, the goal here is to get a firm understanding of your vision for the site. Once submitted your get 2 revisions.

  • 4. We Inspire You

    It's our turn. We take everything that you have given and shown us and we present to you a direction sheet. This sheet will have the color scheme, fonts, your logo, and any prominent images that will be featured on your site. If this website was a house, this would be where we lay the foundation. Once submitted you will get 2 revisions.

  • 5. Back to the lab again

    It's time for us to get creative AF. This is the step where we get our creative design juices flowing and begin to arrange your site into a one of a kind design. This is where we build the walls and set the layout for the house (house analogy throwback).

  • 6. Develop

    This is where the magic happens. Your site will go from informative to a work of art. We'll put in functionality, style, and SEO. Time to decorate the house.

  • 7. QA and Testing

    QA is fancy computer terminology for quality assurance. Essentially we click on ALL THE THINGS and spell check everything. We make sure everything works on your site so that your customers won't have any issues when the site is launched. Once we send the final product to you, your get 3 revisions.

  • 8. Back to the bar

    Once your site goes live, we're gonna take you back to the bar to celebrate and de-brief. We'll give you materials for notifying your customers of your new site, discuss a maintence plan, give you all sorts of consults, and finally celebrate your success over a cold beer.
